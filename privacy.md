Lumo values your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the bot.

1. **Data Collection**  
   Lumo does **not collect, store, or share any personal data**. The bot only processes temporary interaction data necessary for responding to commands (for example, command messages and context).

2. **Data Sharing**  
   No data is shared with third parties or external services.

3. **Data Retention**  
   No data is stored after commands are executed. All interactions are handled in real-time.

4. **User Rights**  
   Since no data is stored, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete.
